Main Task

What Is Connected to Teen Mental Health?

Exploring Patterns in a National Survey

You're a data journalist at a national news outlet. Your editor has assigned you a feature story on a question that's been in the headlines: what is connected to teen mental health?

You've been given a dataset of 200 teenagers compiled from the National Survey of Children's Health (NSCH). Each data point represents one teen and includes information about their:

  • age
  • sex/gender
  • daily screen time
  • difficulty making friends
  • sleep hours
  • physical activity
  • and whether they have ever been diagnosed with depression or anxiety

Your job is to explore this data using the interactive visualization interface and choose 10 teens to feature as the human core of your story, the ones you'll draft your article around.

Use the filters, chart, and details panel to explore before you decide. Once you submit your 10, you won't be able to change them.
